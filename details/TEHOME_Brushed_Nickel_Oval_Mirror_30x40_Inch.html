Elevate Your Bathroom Aesthetic with the TEHOME Brushed Nickel Oval Mirror

This elegant mirror is designed to add a touch of sophistication to your bathroom or any space that demands a refined touch. Crafted from premium stainless steel and TSCA certified MDF, it's built to withstand the challenges of a humid environment without rust or corrosion.

With its capsule-shaped frame and minimalist design, this oval mirror seamlessly integrates into modern or retro bathroom setups, enhancing your vanity with a clean, contemporary look. The 1-inch depth of the frame adds visual balance without taking up unnecessary space, making it ideal for small or spacious bathrooms.

Choose from a variety of finishes, including brushed nickel, polished nickel, and champagne gold, to match your bathroom's color scheme and personal style. The mirror's 30x40 inch size provides generous space for grooming routines while maintaining a sleek and functional profile.

Installation is made easy with pre-mounted D-ring clips and mounting tools included. The anti-distortion 1/6'' thick silver glass ensures that every reflection is clear and true to your appearance, offering a superior viewing experience.

Whether you're updating your bathroom, adding a statement piece to your powder room, or enhancing your entryway, the TEHOME Brushed Nickel Oval Mirror is the perfect choice for those who value both style and durability.
