Sleek 24-inch Black Metal Framed Round Wall Mirror

Add a touch of modern elegance to your space with this stylish and functional wall mirror. Perfect for entryways, bathrooms, or living rooms, it brings clarity, sophistication, and durability to your home decor.

Design & Performance

Featuring HD float glass, this mirror offers a crystal clear, distortion-free reflection, ensuring every detail is visible with precision. Its 24-inch round shape is ideal for small spaces, and the super-slim depth enhances the overall aesthetic.

The modern design is highlighted by sleek, clean lines and an elegant black metal frame that complements contemporary and minimalist interiors. Its lightweight construction makes it easy to install and move, without compromise on quality.

Built for Longevity

Constructed from durable aluminum alloy and thickened baking paint, this mirror is crafted to last. It offers excellent resistance to corrosion, rust, and wear, making it perfect for high-moisture areas like bathrooms.

Each mirror comes with a 3-year warranty and includes all necessary mounting hardware for safe and easy installation. The mirror is packed in rigid packaging to ensure it arrives in excellent condition, ready for use.
